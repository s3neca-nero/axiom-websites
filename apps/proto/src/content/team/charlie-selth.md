Charlie Selth is Proto Axiom's Chief of Staff, providing strategic and operational support to Anthony Liveris (CEO).

Charlie is also the Co-Founder of an alternative asset management firm where, at 21 years of age, he raised $10m for the platform's inaugural investment product which invests on behalf of some of Australia's leading family offices. He is also Co-Founder of Sweat Capital, a media platform for young high performers playing the long game.

Charlie holds a Bachelor of Commerce (BCom) from the University of Queensland.

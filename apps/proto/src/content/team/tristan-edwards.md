Tristan has had a 25-year career, highlighted by co-founding and serving as the first CEO of Boston-based Life Biosciences, the world's leading human longevity company, making drugs to treat causes of ageing.

He previously spent 18 years in global funds management spanning Australia, London, Hong Kong, and Singapore, trading all asset classes with a focus on Asian equities (Goldman Sachs Asset Management, Brevan Howard Asset Management, Trafalgar Capital, Mosaic Asset Management).

Tristan is the co-founder of Entheos Labs, J-Foods, and multiple other university spinouts.

Tom is the Managing Director of Dragoman, a global country and political risk advisory firm based in Melbourne. He is a political scientist with an economics and finance background.

Tom is one of the Australian Prime Minister's nominees to the APEC Business Advisory Council. He also serves on the Advisory Board of the Centre for Arab and Islamic Studies at the Australian National University and chairs the Australia Saudi Business Council.

Tom was the Non-Executive Chairman of Dow Chemical (Australia) and a Senior Advisor to the Executive Leadership Team. Prior, he was the President of Corporate Development at BHP, where he designed and implemented worldwide political risk analysis methodologies and strategies.
